welcome to my village

There was a small village surrounded by mountains and lush green fields. The people there were hardworking and friendly. In the morning, as the first rays of the sun touched the fields, the whole village seemed to awaken with new energy. Children played along the edges of the fields, while the elders sat under the trees, sharing stories. The air in the village carried a unique peace that gets lost in the noise and chaos of the city.
